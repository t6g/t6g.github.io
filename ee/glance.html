First Glance Review Checklist for Subdivision Plans

Environmental Engineering Department, Chesterfield VA

  • tentative conditions/preliminary plat?
  • VSMP compliance criteria
      • Section locations shown accompanied by all calculations
    • Minimum 5-foot contours with incremental drainage areas showing acreage contributions to each point of entry to drainage system. Drainage area plan shall be 1"=50' unless otherwise approved
    • Off-site hydrology must be included in the plan assembly.
    • To include existing ground, left/right offsets along RW, sags/crests, percent slope, storm sewer/inlet structures and profiles of stub roads 300 feet beyond property line
    • To include percent of slope, as well as stationing that corresponds to plan view stationing.
    • CBPA compliance note on title sheet. Compliance sheets must include wetlands, RPA limits, BMP design, CBPA/MS4 table, etc.
    • A separate, detached plan sheet must be submitted depicting all existing and proposed roads, vehicle counts, and locations of required streetlights as determined under the guidelines of the Chesterfield County Streetlight Policy as it pertains to new development
