Open Channel Flow Calculator

Introduction

Open Channel Flow Calculator is a web application for normal and critical flow calculation for triangular, rectangular, trapezoidal, parabolic, elliptical, arch, and irregular channels or closed conduits using Manning's equation. Details are referred to Open Channel Flow with Manning's equation.

An early version is available for triangular, rectangular, trapezoidal, parabolic, elliptical, and arch.

Notes

When a parameter except discharge is changed in the input, Calculator recalculates discharge and other variables.

When discharge is changed in the input, Calculator calculates and saves the normal depth. The discharge is not saved, therefore, the final discharge may be slightly different from the input discharge.
